Fix Your Flag

FYF

Now you don't need to hang up the world-map on the wall and tack with the pin. With this web-site you are able to fix your own flag to the country you've been. Also you can fill up your own color to the country you've been. It's not about your own satisfaction. Compete with others and show how many countries you've been
